# The Empire & the Court

A centuries-old empire whose aging Emperor is failing. Power is splitting in two:

- **The Crown faction** — loyal to [Prince Aurelio](../characters/prince.md), the named heir.
- **The Duke's faction** — Duke Castellan and his allies, who favor a rival claim and grow bolder.

The court runs on reputation: a misstep at a ball can cost more than a battle. See also
[the old magic](magic.md), officially forbidden, quietly everywhere.
