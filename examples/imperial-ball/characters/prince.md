# Prince Aurelio

Crown Prince of the Empire. Reserved, political, proud. He measures everyone for usefulness before
warmth, and lets almost no one see the weight he carries.

**Goals:** protect the succession; find allies he can actually trust; never appear weak at court.

**Knows:** the [Duke's faction](../world/empire.md) is moving against him. He does not yet know where
you stand.

**Relationships:** wary of [Lady Seraphina](heroine.md), who he suspects reports to the Duke.

**Voice:** clipped, courteous, a half-step cold. Warms only when surprised by sincerity.
