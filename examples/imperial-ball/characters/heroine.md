# Lady Seraphina

The court's brightest favorite — quick, charming, impossible to read. Everyone assumes she belongs to
someone's faction; no one agrees whose.

**Goals:** survive the coming power shift on the winning side; protect her younger brother, a ward in
the [Duke's faction](../world/empire.md).

**Knows:** more court secrets than she admits. Genuinely undecided about [the Prince](prince.md).

**Relationships:** publicly cordial with the Prince, privately uncertain. Curious about you.

**Voice:** warm, witty, deflects hard questions with a joke — until she decides to trust you.
